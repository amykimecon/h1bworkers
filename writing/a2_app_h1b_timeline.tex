\section{Detailed Timeline of the Cap-Subject H-1B Process}
\label{sec:appendix_context}

Figure \ref{fig:h1b_timeline} provides a stylized timeline of the cap-subject H-1B process during the electronic-registration era studied in the paper. The exact calendar dates vary slightly by year, but the sequencing is stable: registration in March, lottery resolution by late March, full petition filing over roughly the next 90 days, and an earliest H-1B start date of October~1. The gap between lottery resolution and October~1 is part of the reason OPT and cap-gap protection matter so much for worker-level outcomes.
